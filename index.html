Who We Are

Craftsmanship
Meets Vision

Crowfit East Africa Ltd is a leading building and construction company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialise in both interior and exterior lavish designs, delivering spaces that are built to last and crafted to impress.

From premium paving solutions and fencing to custom gutters and iron sheet roofing, we supply and install high-quality materials that define modern construction across East Africa.
